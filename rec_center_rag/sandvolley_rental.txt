Sand Volleyball Complex Rental Fees:

NKU Departments or Recognized Student Organizations (with all participants as NKU students, faculty, and staff):
- Not assessed a rental fee for reservations.

Rental Fees for Other Groups (Non-NKU Students/Departments):
- Must provide a comprehensive liability insurance policy with NKU named as the insured.
- Insurance policy needs to be for $1 million of coverage.
